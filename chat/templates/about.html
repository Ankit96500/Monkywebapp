About Monky:

Monky, the ultimate online college directory and communication platform designed to connect students and teachers seamlessly. With this, students and teachers can easily find and interact with each other via chat, creating a vibrant profiles according to their preferences.

Real-Time Messaging: Engage in real-time conversations through our intuitive chat interface.

User Profiles: Each user a personalized profile showcasing their academic background, interests, college, courses and areas of expertise. Dive into detailed profiles to gain insights into fellow peers or instructors.

User-Friendly Search: Navigate through our extensive directory effortlessly, with search filters allowing users to find specific students or teachers based on criteria such as college ,courses, or username.

Online Status Indicator: Monky allows you to see who's currently active and available for communication. you'll have a clear indication of when it's the right time to reach out and engage in conversation.
